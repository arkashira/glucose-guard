# Customer Journey Map

## Phase 1: Aware
**Trigger Event:** Diabetes patients or healthcare providers become aware of the limitations and discomfort of traditional invasive blood sugar monitoring methods.
**Friction Points:**
- Lack of awareness about non-invasive monitoring options.
- Skepticism about the accuracy of non-invasive methods.
**User Emotions:**
- Frustration with current monitoring methods.
- Hope for a better solution.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Educational content highlighting the benefits of non-invasive monitoring.
- Testimonials from early adopters.
**Metric:** Number of website visits and downloads of educational materials.

## Phase 2: Consider
**Trigger Event:** Patients or healthcare providers actively seek non-invasive blood sugar monitoring solutions.
**Friction Points:**
- Overwhelming number of options and claims.
- Concerns about cost and insurance coverage.
**User Emotions:**
- Overwhelmed by choices.
- Cautious optimism.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Detailed comparison guides and cost-benefit analyses.
- Free trials or demo sessions.
**Metric:** Number of demo requests and comparison guide downloads.

## Phase 3: Try
**Trigger Event:** Decision to try GlucoseGuard based on positive reviews and demo experiences.
**Friction Points:**
- Initial setup and integration with existing healthcare systems.
- Learning curve for using the new technology.
**User Emotions:**
- Excitement about trying a new solution.
- Anxiety about potential difficulties.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Seamless onboarding process with step-by-step guides.
- 24/7 customer support for troubleshooting.
**Metric:** Time taken to complete setup and initial usage feedback scores.

## Phase 4: Adopt
**Trigger Event:** Positive experience during the trial period leads to full adoption.
**Friction Points:**
- Integration with daily routines and existing healthcare protocols.
- Ongoing cost considerations.
**User Emotions:**
- Relief and satisfaction with the new solution.
- Commitment to long-term use.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Personalized usage tips and health insights.
- Loyalty programs and discounts for long-term users.
**Metric:** Percentage of trial users who become paying customers.

## Phase 5: Expand
**Trigger Event:** Satisfied users recommend GlucoseGuard to others or seek additional features.
**Friction Points:**
- Limited awareness among potential new users.
- Desire for advanced features and integrations.
**User Emotions:**
- Pride in using an innovative solution.
- Eagerness to explore additional benefits.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Referral programs with incentives.
- Regular updates and new feature rollouts.
**Metric:** Number of referrals and adoption of new features.
