## Customer Segments
- **Type 1 & Type 2 Diabetes patients** (≈ 34 M in the US, 5 M in Thailand) seeking painless, continuous glucose monitoring.  
- **Endocrinologists & primary‑care clinics** that need reliable, non‑invasive data for treatment decisions.  
- **Health‑insurance providers** looking to reduce costly diabetes complications through better adherence.  
- **Corporate wellness programs** (large employers, especially in high‑risk regions) aiming to lower employee health costs.  

## Value Propositions
- **Truly non‑invasive monitoring** – no finger‑sticks, skin‑penetrating sensors, or consumables.  
- **AI‑driven accuracy** – predictive algorithms achieve < 10 % MARD (Mean Absolute Relative Difference) compared to CGM standards.  
- **Real‑time alerts & actionable insights** via mobile app, reducing hypoglycemia events by an estimated 30 %.  
- **Lower total cost of ownership** – no recurring sensor replacements; device price $300 USD (≈ 10,200 THB) vs. $1,000 USD for conventional CGM kits.  

## Channels
- **Direct‑to‑Consumer (DTC) e‑commerce** – company website & major marketplaces (Amazon, Lazada).  
- **Medical device distributors** – partnerships with established distributors in US, EU, and ASEAN.  
- **Hospital & clinic procurement** – sales teams targeting endocrinology departments and diabetes centers.  
- **Tele‑health & digital‑health platforms** – integration as a hardware add‑on for remote monitoring services.  

## Customer Relationships
- **Subscription‑based SaaS** – monthly $20 USD (≈ 680 THB) for analytics, alerts, and clinician dashboard.  
- **24/7 digital support & AI chatbot** for troubleshooting and education.  
- **Community & coaching programs** – webinars, peer forums, and personalized lifestyle recommendations.  
- **Enterprise account managers** for insurers and corporate wellness clients, providing quarterly health‑outcome reports.  

## Revenue Streams
- **Device sales** – $300 USD (≈ 10,200 THB) per unit; projected 50 k units in Year 1.  
- **Monthly SaaS subscription** – $20 USD (≈ 680 THB) per active user; 70 % conversion of device buyers.  
- **Data licensing** (anonymized glucose trends) to pharma & research firms – $0.05 USD per data point.  
- **White‑label OEM deals** – bulk pricing for partner brands (e.g., $250 USD per unit).
